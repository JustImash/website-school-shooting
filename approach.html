Bullying and how to deal with it Some students are bullied by others. Classmates, teachers, friends, parents, all of them are to blame for the psychological deviation of the killer. We believe that everyone should be tolerant towards each other. Students and teachers of the school must follow the rules of showing tolerance towards each other. There must be a framework that every teacher and student must adhere to. If the rule was violated not only by the student, but also by the teacher, the witness must immediately tell the leadership of the educational institution about the incident. After all, bullying is one of the fundamental factors in all these incidents. A person has been subjected to physical and psychological abuse for a long time. People need to become more attentive and try to pay attention to the minimum difficulties of others in order to support them. Conclusion: Schools must take strong measures to prevent any form of bullying, regardless of who the bully is, the student or the teacher. The school must conduct anonymous surveys to find students who are subject to bullying. How should parents prevent school shootings? Neither parent encouraged their child to commit murder or helped plan attacks. But it is worth admitting that many parents knew about the aggressive prejudices of their children, but still turned a blind eye to it. To reduce the risk of not only attacks, but also dangerous behavior in general, parents can: 1. Be interested in your child's life. It sounds primitive, but you, as a parent, should know the child. About his feelings, experiences, goals and interests. Find out through conversation where the child is going, with whom he is friends, what sites he visits and what he posts in his social networks. If you find out that the child has a passion for violence, weapons, etc., you should seek professional help and not ignore that. 2. Pay attention to bad reviews about a child. Imagine, you were told that your son or daughter attacked classmates. Or draw a drawing where someone is killing somebody. In case of a complaint, seek professional help or try to sort it out yourself since this kind of problem will be a cause of future problems. 3. Store weapons in places where the child has no access. If you have a weapon, keep it in a safe. Never give Children access to it. Even if you and your children are fond of hunting for example. Counteraction and minimization of consequences

Referring to the Better Relationships program, we hope that it will be able to prevent the appearance of an aggressive environment, respectively, the appearance of the shooter's cheekbones. But unfortunately in our time we cannot be absolutely sure. In any case, there are always exceptions to the rules, as we discussed earlier, there is a category of shooters who are mentally ill. Their motives and reasons are unknown to anyone, therefore, it is not possible to prevent them at the stage of the formation. This is an example from many other situations. We still do not exclude that these rules will also to some extent help in other attacks on the school that are not related to school shooting, such as terrorist attacks or hostage-taking. After doing a little research, we came to the conclusion that most people do not know the rules of behavior in such situations at all. Even the most persistent can panic and commit rash actions that lead to disastrous results. MOTIVATION The main goal of this program is to identify the shooter and to minimize such accidents. Also, one of the most important goals is to educate people about the rules of behavior when attacking. We will analyze: 1) Necessary requirements for educational institutions in order to prevent and minimize school shootings 2) Rules of Conduct for Attacks on a School 3) Revealing signs of intent REQUIREMENTS FOR EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS 1) Availability of qualified and professional security guards with non-lethal-traumatic weapons 2) CCTV camera system 3) The presence of a panic button 4) Access control in educational institutions 5) The presence of a metal detector 1 SECURITY: Most often, various violations are observed at this point, since the person holding this position is not suitable mentally or physically. The HR department must carefully select employees for this position. A person must be physically strong in order to put up decisive resistance to the attacker. Also, he must be extremely careful, because most often the school shooter smuggles weapons secretly. In such situations, the deductive and analytical skills of the guard will be required. The number of security personnel is also a very important factor, everything should depend on the size of the institution and the number of students. But the rule "The more the better" - works great in this situation. Each guard must be equipped with self-defense equipment. Not lethal, but varying in severity. It is not necessary to have it with you, but it should be at hand. The following self-defense means such as rubber truncheons, traumatic pistols, gas cans, shockers and teasers are suitable for this task. The guards are the first and only physical shield between the threat and the children until the force team arrives. CAMERA SYSTEM: Cameras are the eyes and ears of the school, they are closely related to the security. The sooner the guards know about the incident, the more time it will take to take action. The cameras also help to identify the number of victims, the location of the shooter and provide an overview of the whole picture. After the incident, cameras will help the investigation to reveal the full history of the incident. ALARM BUTTON: Performs the same function as cameras, notifies everyone about the danger. Also, when pressed, there will be an automatic call to the rescue services. The presence of such buttons is a mandatory requirement for all institutions. NOTIFICATION SYSTEM: To identify the danger is the floor of the problem, the second part of the problem is to promptly notify all people about the problem. Loudspeakers, alarms, social media chats, applications, etc. are suitable for this concept. Anything that can alert you to danger. Articulating the hazard and coordinating the actions of children is the way to avoid casualties. Therefore, many agencies should consider an effective communication method. PASS MODE: Each educational institution must control the passage of people to the school grounds. By passes, QR codes or other means. Search and examination of suspicious people is included in this list. Keeping the shooter out of the way will help buy time or completely neutralize the threat at the entrance. Also included in this category are metal detectors and metal detectors that can help identify huge metal objects such as knives and firearms. 2 RULES OF CONDUCT DURING SCHOOL SHOOTING: INTRODUCTION: We hope that you will never need this information in real life, but in any case, you should definitely read the tips that we give below. They will not only save you, but also the people around you. It is better to know what to do and never face it than not to know what to do when it happens. Forewarned is forearmed. RECOMMENDATIONS FOR ADMINISTRATIONS AND MANUALS OF EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS: Based on our research, we came to the conclusion that absolutely no one knows what to do in such cases, neither the teacher nor the students. How to solve this problem? Our program fully talks about this problem and each of its individual aspects, but we will list other steps that can be taken to minimize the consequences. To discuss with teachers a plan of action in such cases, to conduct trainings. Conduct frequent checks to improve evacuation efficiency. ACTIONS THAT SHOULD NOT BE DONE: We are all human and you should never forget about it, but in this case, the manifestation of mercy can lead to disastrous consequences. DO NOT ATTEMPT TO RELAX THE SHOOTER: We all would like to become the key to solving the problem, to save everyone. But first you need to understand the motives. According to research conducted by psychologists, the condition that the School Shooter is experiencing is similar to that of a suicidal person. They came to die in these schools, they came to kill themselves. This is a completely uncontrollable emotion. It is impossible to persuade a person to lay down their arms if they intend to eventually kill themselves. The success of the negotiations is extremely low, after each subsequent sacrifice the chance of success of the negotiations is significantly reduced, tending to zero. Do not negotiate with the shooter, this puts your life and the lives of others in great danger. For all your kindness, unfortunately you cannot help this person, it is too late. DON'T PANIC: It's hard not to succumb to such an extreme environment where your life is close to death, but just because of this you should stop worrying. You need a sound mind that is interrupted by panic and a huge level of adrenaline. You must realize that panic will not help here, but will only aggravate the situation. There are a couple of techniques that can help you deal with stress and pressure. You should look at the ceiling, then at the floor and mentally understand that you are here and now, in this place, in this moment. With peace of 3 mind, you can make more rational decisions that will help not only you but others to survive as well. DO NOT TAKE A VIDEO: A common mistake among people is recording everything on camera. Many people with the goal of becoming famous, instead of following the steps below, start filming a video. This is very dangerous because you are putting your life and not only your own danger. RULES OF ACTION FOR SСHOOL SHOOTING Hearing shots or alarms, you have very little time to think and make the right decision. But after reading this material, you will be fully aware of all the nuances and will act correctly. There is an effective strategy for surviving mass shootings. Strategy: Run, Hide, Fight. 1) Run - quickly leave dangerous territory 2) Find a safe place and wait for rescue 3) Provide resistance Below we will look at each of the sub-points and explain all aspects of when you need to adhere to a particular strategy. RUN: WHEN TO USE: If you can roughly understand the location of the shooter, then you should leave dangerous territory as soon as possible, if possible. When moving, you should not run along the same trajectory, you should move from cover to cover to warn people about the danger along the way. Do it in clear, short phrases. People under stress do not accept long commands. Try not to draw the shooter's attention or get caught in his field of vision WHEN TO NOT USE: If the process of flight exposes a person to more danger than that which is now or is expected in the near future, then this strategy must be abandoned. You should not run if the school shooter is next to you and your escape route is within the shooter's target area. You cannot run in dense groups, this will attract extra attention. Don't run in open space. HIDE: If you did not manage to leave the dangerous territory, then proceed to this step. It is required to find a safe room with a lot of furniture: chairs, tables, and wardrobes. 1) The first step is to barricade, the more people the more effective and faster this process will be, and it can also be improved by conducting trainings. Barricades can be done in a variety of ways. Use straps and other ropes to tie the doorknob and door into a heavy object, and move tables, chairs and cabinets close to the door. 4 2) Make it look like an empty class, hide, turn off the lights. Also an important point is to turn off the sound on mobile devices.As previously dismantled the school shooter acts chaotically, without noticing anyone in the office, he will not take measures to penetrate. 3) Lie on the floor. You have to understand: a person shoots either from the shoulder or from the belt. We must be below the line of fire. Therefore, the teacher's task is to order the children to lie on the floor in the first seconds. The attacker will take time to realize that the children are below. FIGHT: Considering this situation, it would not be the best solution to enter into direct confrontation, but when this remains your only path to survival, you should be aware of the correct technique of action. It is worth thinking about entering a confrontation at the previous stage and being ready for anything. Most often, the shooters' weapons are long smooth-bore carbines, in the US there can be any weapon. It is also worth remembering edged weapons. Tactics should be adapted to the type of weapon. 1) Use the environment to your advantage. Hide in corners, doorways, etc. Any means for a covert attack and disarmament. 2) Attack in a crowd, quick coordinated actions of a large number of people will be able to disarm the shooter. Divide everyone's roles and actions. 3) If the opponent is right-handed, dive and dodge to the left and vice versa. 4) Consider who is behind you, because when trying to attack and disarm, the shooter can make shots that can injure those standing behind. Aim the muzzle at the ceiling. WHEN TO NOT USE: 1) If you are not confident in your abilities and panic seized you 2) When confrontation can be avoided NOTE: Use only at the very end, with a hopeless option. Don't be a hero. RESCUE STAGE: If you were able to leave the dangerous area or wait for the arrival of special bodies, follow the steps below. Execute all special services commands. When leaving dangerous territory, raise your hands and keep them at head level to indicate that you are not a threat. Do not make sudden movements Provide all important information that will help the specialized authorities in resolving the problem. Armament, number of shooters, number of victims and wounded, approximate location Don't stand near the windows 5 FIRST AID: Most deaths do not occur immediately, the main cause of death is painful shock and bleeding. There is no way you can help a person with a painful shock. To help a person, you must know a number of rules about the first one in case of a bullet wound. It all depends on where the bullet hits. 1) Anesthesia (nalbuphine, ketorolac, dexalgin, analgin intramuscularly). 2) Applying an aseptic dressing to the gunshot wound, preferably 3) using a sterile dressing bag; stopping external bleeding (pressing dressing, applying a tourniquet); 3) Immobilization of the limb. After completing these procedures, you can stop bleeding and save a person's life. But save your life only if it is safe for your own life and does not endanger you.
