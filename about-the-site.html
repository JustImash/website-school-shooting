A school shooting is an attack at an educational institution, such as a primary school, secondary school, or university, involving the use of firearms. Let's start with the simplest What associations do you have with the word school? We also asked this question to ourselves and decided to ask the opinion of others. Most people said school is about knowledge, friends, family and protection to them. Many have focused on safety, because this is one of the most important points. Each parent boldly sends their children to school, being confident in their safety. It is not surprising because the school is the guarantor of trust and protection.



But unfortunately in our time nothing can be predicted Recently, there has been an increase in the number of attacks on a school with the use of firearms and other types of weapons. The horrific statistics of attacks on schools on the slide. Approximately 1,360 children have been killed in the United States since 1970. The number itself is terrifying; not everyone can imagine it To make it clear the real extent of the problem, Imagine 55 yellow American buses full of children. Most often these are innocent children who have nothing to do with the shooter. In fact, 101 school shootings happened throughout this year in the United States. This was the highest school shooting rate since 1970. Why we chose this theme We have several reasons why we decided to select this problem. These actions are becoming more and more popular around the world. Every year on average between 25 and 30 students die due to school shootings and this number is increasing Moreover, during our project work , we used a website that shows the total number of school shootings for 2021. There were 101 and in point of fact, in a little over a week, the number of school shootings rose by 5 and reached 106 accidents in the US. This event supported our vision and motivated us to work harder and more to represent the whole situation and show the urgency of the problem. Actually, these two points are the main purpose of the project.
